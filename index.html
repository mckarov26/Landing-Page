Famous Cuisines of the Philippines

  • "Lechon Baboy" is a popular Filipino dish consisting of a whole roasted pig. It's centerpiece of many celebrations and gatherings, known for its crispy skin and succulent meat.

  • It is called "Bulalo" in the Philippines, it has rich and flavorful broth, tender beef shanks, and marrow-filled bones.

  • Known as "Sizzling Sisig with egg" is a delicious Filipino dish made from chopped and seasoned pork face, ears and liver. Enjoyed as a main course or as a tasty appetizer.

  • Filipino spring rolls or known as "Lumpia", imagine biting into crispy, golden-brown spring roll filled with a savory medley of vegetables, meat, and spices - a burst of flavor in every bite.
